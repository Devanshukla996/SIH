Popular Destinations

Betla National Park

Home to diverse wildlife including tigers, elephants, and various species of deer and birds.

Dassam Falls

A magnificent waterfall located near Ranchi, known for its natural beauty and serene surroundings.

Jagannath Temple

A beautiful temple in Ranchi, resembling the famous Jagannath Temple of Puri.

Rich Cultural Heritage

Tribal Art Forms

Jharkhand is home to numerous tribal communities, each with their unique art forms. Sohrai and Khovar paintings are particularly famous, depicting nature and daily life in vibrant colors.

Traditional Festivals

The state celebrates various festivals with great enthusiasm. Sarhul, Karma, and Tusu Parab are among the most important festivals that showcase the rich cultural heritage of Jharkhand.

Local Cuisine

Jharkhand's cuisine is as diverse as its culture. Traditional dishes like Thekua, Rugra, Bamboo Shoot Curry, and Handia (rice beer) offer a unique culinary experience.
